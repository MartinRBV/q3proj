Instructions:

  1. This is a spinoff of the classic Rock-Paper-Scissors game, so the normal rules will apply.
  2. Players can either Attack (with Rock, Paper, or Scissors), Block, or Charge.
  3. Charging gives a player 2 counts of charge (Max 5 charge per player.
  4. Attacking requires at least 2 charge to use but only costs 1 charge.
  5. Defending blocks any attack but cannot be used twice in a row.
  6. All Players must charge on the 1st Turn.
  7. Being attacked while charging awards the attacker 1 point.
  8. The first to three points wins!

Extra Tips and Tricks: Charge-Defend-Attack

  • Charge-Defend-Attack is the most successful strategy we've tried in the game.
  • After charging on Turn 1, defending will be the safest option so as to avoid an attack or break even if the bot defends.
  • Successfully blocking on Turn 2 puts you at an advantage that allows you to charge freely without the bot attacking.
  • This forces the bot to either defend or charge, allowing you to win if the bot charges.
  • If the bot defends, simply repeat the whole process until you win.
  • If you have extra charge to spare, keep pressing attacks and be aggressive.
  • Keep in mind, however, that the bot may still beat you in normal Rock-Paper-Scissors.
  • In general, try to keep a higher charge level than the bot at all times.
